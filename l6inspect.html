You emerge into the hole, which is clustered and damp. But: there's sunlight! Or some kind of light. It's filtering through the gaps in the stone wall. First, you see nothing but grey, but then start to notice a pattern in the rocks. Some are darker than others. It's beginning to look familiar... like a pattern? Maybe the stones can be moved around a certain way... Some of them look damp, like they're ready to crumble.
